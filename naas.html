The Model Explained

What Is Network as a Service?

Network as a Service (NaaS) is a subscription-based model where networking hardware, software, monitoring, support, and lifecycle management are delivered as a single managed service — instead of traditional upfront purchases.

Clients no longer need to buy, manage, or refresh network equipment. ANJ designs, installs, monitors, and maintains the network for a predictable monthly fee.

Who Is NaaS For?

  • ✓Growing businesses that want enterprise networking without CapEx
  • ✓Multi-site organizations needing centralized visibility and control
  • ✓Data center environments requiring consistent performance and uptime
  • ✓Regulated industries that need documented, managed network infrastructure
