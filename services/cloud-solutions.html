Cloud & Hybrid Solutions

ANJ's cloud services cover the full adoption journey — from architecture design and migration planning through secure remote access, backup, disaster recovery, and ongoing optimization. We work with your timeline and requirements, not a vendor roadmap.

Challenges We Address

Common Problems We Solve

⚠

Security & Compliance in the Cloud

Moving to cloud without proper architecture and controls creates new security risks and compliance gaps — especially for regulated data.

💸

Cloud Cost Sprawl

Unmanaged cloud environments balloon in cost rapidly. Without proper governance, cloud spend becomes unpredictable and wasteful.

🔌

Connectivity & Access Control

Secure remote access, site-to-cloud connectivity, and access control across hybrid environments require careful architectural planning.

What We Do

Our Cloud Solutions Services

🏗

Cloud & Hybrid Architecture Design

Architecture design for cloud-first, hybrid, and multi-cloud environments — built for security, performance, and compliance from the start.

🔐

Secure Remote Access

VPN, ZTNA, and secure access architectures that connect remote users and branch sites to cloud and on-premises resources safely.

💾

Backup & Disaster Recovery

Cloud-based backup and DR solutions with tested recovery procedures — ensuring your data is protected and recoverable.

⚙

Cloud Optimization & Cost Management

Cloud environment reviews, rightsizing, reserved instance planning, and governance policies to control and optimize cloud spend.
